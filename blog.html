Here is a short poem about HTML

        
HTML is where websites start,
With tags and code, it plays its part.
Head and body, neat and clear,
Tells the browser what to show here.

Images, links, and lines of text,
Build a page from start to next.
Though simple, it's the web's first frame,
And every site owes it the same.

Poem for HTML Text Formatting

HTML Text gets dressed so neat,
With tags that make it bold or sweet.
<b> for bold, it stands out strong,
<i> adds style, like in a song.

<u> will underline the line,
While <mark> makes highlights shine.
<sup> and <sup> play up and down,
Formatting text like a digital crown.
